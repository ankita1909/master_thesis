CLK_CLOCK_DIV_R

Field CLK_CLOCK_DIV reader - PDM CLK (FPDM_CLK) (1st divider): This configures a frequency of PDM CLK. The configured frequency is used to operate PDM core. I.e. the frequency is input to MCLKQ_CLOCK_DIV register. Note: configure a frequency of PDM CLK as lower than or equal 50MHz with this divider.
