Field PARITY_ENABLED reader - Parity generation enabled (‘1’) or not (‘0’). Only applicable in standard UART submodes. In SmartCard submode, parity generation is always enabled through hardware. In IrDA submode, parity generation is always disabled through hardware

PARITY_R

Field PARITY reader - Parity bit. When ‘0’, the transmitter generates an even parity. When ‘1’, the transmitter generates an odd parity. Only applicable in standard UART and SmartCard submodes.

PARITY_W

Field PARITY writer - Parity bit. When ‘0’, the transmitter generates an even parity. When ‘1’, the transmitter generates an odd parity. Only applicable in standard UART and SmartCard submodes.
