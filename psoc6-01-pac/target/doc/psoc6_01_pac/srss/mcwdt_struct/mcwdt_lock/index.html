Multi-Counter Watchdog Counter Lock Register

Structs

MCWDT_LOCK_R

Field MCWDT_LOCK reader - Prohibits writing control and configuration registers related to this MCWDT when not equal 0 (as specified in the other register descriptions). Requires at least two different writes to unlock. Note that this field is 2 bits to force multiple writes only. Each MCWDT has a separate local lock. LFCLK settings are locked by the global WDT_LOCK register, and this register has no effect on that.
